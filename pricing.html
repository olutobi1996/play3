Football Training Prices & Details

Clear, simple pricing with flexible options for kids who love to play, learn, and improve.

Sessions are limited to 16 kids max per hour to ensure quality coaching and attention.

Monthly subscription pricing is available and can be discussed individually.

Play3 Football • FAQs

Everything you need before you play

What is the Play3 environment like?

Every young baller at Play3 inspires one another through fun, passion and full commitment. We promote positive energy, awareness and confidence on and off the pitch, always leading with love, never fear. Play3 is more than football; it’s a family where every player feels supported, encouraged and empowered.

How many players are allowed?

We cap each hour at 16 players to guarantee fast gameplay, quick rotations and a high number of touches per player.

Do you offer monthly subscriptions?

Yes, monthly passes are available. Pricing depends on how many sessions you want per week. Speak to us directly to build your subscription.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is on-site parking directly at the complex, making drop-offs and pick-ups easy and safe for parents. The car park is monitored and conveniently close to the pitch area.

Where do sessions take place?

We play at Chiswick School, Burlington Lane, W4 3UN, on high-quality, well-maintained pitches perfect for fast small-sided football.

When are sessions available?

Sessions run every Monday and Wednesday evening. Additional days may be added depending on demand.

What age/skill level is Play3 for?

Play3 is designed for every young footballer of any skill, just be open and your 3quency will be raised. All skill levels are welcome.
