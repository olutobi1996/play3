The team behind Play3

Tune into the 3quency, football + futsal coaching

What is Play3?

Tune into the 3quency. Play3 is a coaching brand that fuses the pace and invention of futsal with the heartbeat and pathways of football. We give young players a safe, encouraging space to explore, make mistakes, and level up their game — technically, tactically and mentally.

Play3 exists to reinvent how young players train. By mixing futsal intensity with football IQ, every session becomes a space to experiment, express and grow. Players leave with sharper decision-making, better touches and a deeper feel for the game.

Our coaching model is built on three pillars: freedom, structure and progression. Freedom to try things, structure to build real technique, and steady progression that builds confidence session by session.

Play3 is more than training, it’s a community. We bring families together, celebrate milestones and create an environment where players feel supported, valued and inspired.

If you want coaching that builds skill and joy at the same time, Play3 delivers. We teach control, creativity and game understanding, helping every player unlock new levels on the pitch.

Meet the Play3 coaches

A small, tightly-knit coaching team with professional playing and futsal roots, focused on building confidence, creativity and craft in every young player.

Frank Vincent
Founder & Head Coach
Our very own number 33, energy, passion and relentless positivity. West London raised and QPR bred, Frank learned the hard way that setbacks are part of the journey: from youth football to spells at Barnet and Bournemouth, every obstacle sharpened his focus.
As Head Coach Frank brings structure to flair. He coaches with empathy, pushes players to keep their curiosity, and builds programmes that turn small technical gains into big game confidence. His sessions emphasise resilience, workrate and the joy of playing.
Dario O’Reilly Cocker
Lead Futsal Coach
The captain, connector and the soul of Play3. Acton born and futsal raised, Dario brings that compact, high-skill futsal DNA into every drill. His coaching is about quick touches, sharp decision-making and creative problem solving on the pitch.
Dario leads by example, patient with kids, honest with feedback and brilliant at unlocking a player’s natural instincts. Off the court he builds local culture: community sessions, small tournaments and coaching clinics that keep players engaged and inspired.
Phillippe Ferrari
Technical Coach
The magician from São Paulo, Philli blends South American creativity with a modern coaching approach. Calm, confident and technically excellent, his sessions are where control meets expression: close control, feints and small combinations that make players more dangerous in tight spaces.
Phillippe focuses on first touch, ball mastery and the micro-decisions that split defences. He’s also a patient communicator, adept at turning complex techniques into simple, repeatable progressions for all ages.
Luke Gambin
Senior Coach & Mentor
The international, a technical operator with a lion’s heart. Luke’s journey through the EFL and the Malta national team gives him real-world insight into professional pathways and player development.
He specialises in decision-making under pressure, combination play and transitional moments. Luke also mentors older players on pathways, trials and mindset, blending elite experience with grounded coaching that builds futures.
